* How to use? |
-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------
    -Keyboard
        * CTRL+N        -        deletes everything
        * CTRL+Z        -        undo
        * UP            -        next brush
        * DOWN          -        prev brush

    -Mouse
        * Left          -        paint, =)
        * Scroll        -        next/prev brush
